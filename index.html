Typography with Food

Food, substance consisting essentially of protein, carbohydrates, and fat, and other nutrients used in the body of an organism to sustain growth and vital processes and to furnish energy.
The absorbtion of and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is facilitated by digestion.
Plants, which convert solar energy to food by photosynthesis, are a primary source of food for humans and animals.

Animals that feed on plants often serve as sources of food for other animals.

Food is treated in a number of articles. For a description of the processes of absorption and utilization of food, see nutrition;nutrition; human; digestion; and digestive system,human
